Account Deletion Request

You can request the permanent deletion of your MeetApp account at any time. Once processed, all associated personal data will be removed from our systems.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MeetApp app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Tap on "Delete Account" and confirm your request.

Alternative Option

If you're unable to delete your account from the app, you can send an email to support@meetapp.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and we'll process your request.

What Happens After Deletion

  • All personal data (name, email, account info) will be permanently deleted.
  • Data will be removed within 7 days of request.
  • Some data may be retained if required for legal obligations.
